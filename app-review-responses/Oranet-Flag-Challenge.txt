2. App Purpose
Oranet: Flag Challenge is an educational quiz app that helps users learn and recognize flags of countries around the world. Users are shown a flag and must select the correct country from multiple choices. The app tracks scores, streaks, and overall progress, making it a fun and engaging way to learn world geography.

3. Instructions
- No login or account required. The app is fully functional immediately upon launch.
- "Quiz" tab: A flag is displayed — tap the correct country name from the options provided.
- "Learn" tab: Browse and study flags organized by region.
- "Stats" tab: View your quiz scores, streaks, and learning progress.
- "Settings" tab: Access privacy policy and app information.

4. External Services
The app works fully offline. No external services, APIs, data providers, authentication services, or payment processors are used. All flag images and country data are bundled locally.

5. Regional Differences
The app functions consistently across all regions. No regional differences in features or content.

6. Regulated Industry
Not applicable. This is a general education/trivia app.
