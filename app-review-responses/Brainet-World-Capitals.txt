2. App Purpose
Brainet: World Capitals is an educational app that helps users learn and memorize the capital cities of countries around the world. It provides flashcards with swipe-based learning, multiple-choice quizzes, and a marathon typing mode. The app tracks progress by region, helping users identify which capitals they know and which need more practice.

3. Instructions
- No login or account required. The app is fully functional immediately upon launch.
- "Train" tab: Swipe right if you know the capital, left if you don't. Tap the card to reveal the answer.
- "Quiz" tab: Select the correct capital from four options.
- "Progress" tab: View learning statistics by region.
- "Settings" tab: Access privacy policy and app information.

4. External Services
The app works fully offline. No external services, APIs, data providers, authentication services, or payment processors are used. All country/capital data is bundled locally.

5. Regional Differences
The app functions consistently across all regions. No regional differences in features or content.

Not applicable. This is a general education/trivia app.
