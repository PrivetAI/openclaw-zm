2. App Purpose
Cycle Fasting Timer is a health and fitness app that helps users practice intermittent fasting. Users can select popular fasting protocols (16:8, 18:6, 20:4, etc.), start a fasting timer with visual countdown, and track their fasting history over time. The app provides a simple, distraction-free way to maintain a consistent fasting routine.

3. Instructions
- No login or account required. The app is fully functional immediately upon launch.
- Select a fasting protocol (e.g. 16:8) and tap Start to begin the timer.
- The timer screen shows remaining time with a visual progress indicator.
- View fasting history and streaks in the history/stats section.
- Access settings and privacy policy from the settings tab.

4. External Services
The app works fully offline. No external services, APIs, data providers, authentication services, or payment processors are used. All fasting data is stored locally on the device.

5. Regional Differences
The app functions consistently across all regions. No regional differences in features or content.

6. Regulated Industry
Not applicable. This is a general wellness/lifestyle app. It does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. It is a simple timer tool for tracking fasting intervals.
