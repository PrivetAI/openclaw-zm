## Critical Fix: UILaunchScreen
- ALL 5 apps were missing `UILaunchScreen` in Info.plist
- Without it, apps render at 320x480 (compatibility mode) on iPhone 17
- Fixed by adding `<key>UILaunchScreen</key><dict/>` to each Info.plist
- Also tried LaunchScreen.storyboard but XML was invalid for Xcode on macOS 26.2
- **Must add UILaunchScreen dict to Info.plist for ALL future apps** — add to GUIDE.md
