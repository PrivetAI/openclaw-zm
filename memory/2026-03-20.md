## Redirect Watcher Pattern (Simplified)
- Use `foundCheckDomain` boolean flag + `resolvedURL` tracking
- Check domain appears in TWO places: mid-chain interception + final URL check
- Always `.contains()` for domain checks, never `.hasPrefix()` or exact match
- Never stop chain early with `completionHandler(nil)` ‚Äî causes error, breaks detection
